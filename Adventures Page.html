Why choose us?    

With us beginning this service all the way back in 1998, we have huge experience in activites and what makes them thrilling! Dont believe us? take a look at the list below which shows only a fraction of what we offer.   

Climbing 

With our company offering hundreds of climbing activites across the country people are given a choice in what they want to experience. Whether it be mountain climbing, indoor climbing, etc.         

Sports 

With our company people can be given access to sporting facilites they never could before. We also can offer visits to professional sporting team, given the chance to look into the life of an athlete.  

Caving 

If you want to experience the extreme then look no further then our caving experience! Being tested by many safety companies, we can assure you that this activity is safe and thrilling for those interested.        

 Kayaking

If you are looking for an activity in water then we have the experience for you! Being a loved experience by previous customers we put a lot of effort into making sure you enjoy your time with our kayaking experience.
